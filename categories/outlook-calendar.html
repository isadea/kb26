What Is Outlook Calendar?

To display events and bookings in your Outlook Calendar, you can use one-way Outlook Calendar synchronization. This will move bookings from your Nexudus Calendar to your Outlook Calendar. It won't transfer bookings from your Outlook Calendar to Nexudus.

Setting up Outlook Calendar 

Synchronization is made using iCal feeds. iCal feeds are accessed using URLs that are specific to  information about each of your events and bookings.

Each resource in your Nexudus account has a dedicated Calendar feed which you can use to create a calendar in your Outlook account. You will find these addresses by using the instructions below:

  1. In the side menu, click Inventory >  Booking Resources.

  2. Click the resource that you would like to integrate with your Outlook Calendar.

  3. Scroll down to the Calendar feeds section and copy one of the three links available based on which resources you want to connect and whether or not you want the customer's names to be visible in Outlook. 

Adding Nexudus Calendars to Your Outlook Calendar

Log in to your Outlook calendar, click the File menu and then click Account Settings. 


Click Internet Calendars and add a calendar by clicking New. Use the iCal feeds addresses when Outlook prompts you to enter new calendar details.
