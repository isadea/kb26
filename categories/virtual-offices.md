## What are virtual offices? 
Virtual offices helps businesses and freelancers have a professional presence in your space without having to rent a physical office space.

### Benefits for your space
#### ➡️ New revenue stream with little to no overhead
Offer additional services to customers, creating new income opportunities without needing to get more staff or expand your space. 

#### ➡️ Simplified Management
Easily create and manage customer contracts and streamline handling of mail and parcels.

### Benefits for your customers

#### ➡️ A prime business address 
Customers can use your space address as their business address in a prime location without the traditional cost renting an actual office space.

#### ➡️ Flexible mail handling service
Customers can conveniently get their mail and deliveries sent to your space and collect them when they can. They can also choose more personalized options like scanning, forwarding, or secure long term storage.
