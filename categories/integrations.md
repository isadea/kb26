We integrate with many other platforms to let you use your favorite tools alongside {{variable.Nexudus}}. Our integrations range from[ access control systems](/v3/docs/access-control){target="_blank"} to [video conferencing](/v3/docs/video-conferencing){target="_blank"} and [accounting systems ](/v3/docs/accounting){target="_blank"} to [coffee brewing services](/v3/docs/other){target="_blank"}. 

You can access all our integrations by clicking **Settings > Integrations** {{variable.OnTheAdminPanel}}.

![Integrations_Path.png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/Integrations_Path.png){height="" width=""}

The **Integrations** page is divided into sections to help you easily browse the available options. The integrations that are already enabled are always listed at the top of the page under **Active integrations**.

![Integrations_Page.png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/Integrations_Page.png){height="" width=""}

For more details on each integration and setup guides, click on any of the sections in the navigation menu on the left-hand side of the page.
