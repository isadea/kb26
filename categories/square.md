## What is {{variable.Square}} ePOS?
The integration with {{variable.Square}} ePOS let you to transfer selected products from your {{variable.Nexudus}} inventory as well as all your Nexudus customers to your {{variable.Square}} ePOS. All Nexudus products you sell via your {{variable.Square}} ePOS to Nexudus customers are automatically synced in Nexudus.

## How the {{variable.Square}} ePOS integration works
You connect {{variable.Square}} ePOS to your {{variable.Nexudus}} account to connect your inventory to {{variable.Square}}. You can choose to connect all your products or only a select few.  you sell a Nexudus product to a Nexudus customer via the customers purchase a product through {{variable.Square}} and payments are received via {{variable.Square}} readers, the transaction is automatically synced in {{variable.Nexudus}} where an invoice and the related payment are also recorded.

## FAQ
### Can I use Nexudus with Square Payments? 
No, Nexudus only connects to Square ePOS. 

### Is stock automatically updated? 
Yes, as long as the product you're selling through {{variable.Square}} is synced to Nexudus. We'll automatically adjust the product stock in Nexudus as you sell it through {{variable.Square}}, even if you sell that product to someone who isn't a customer {{variable.OnTheAdminPanel}}.
