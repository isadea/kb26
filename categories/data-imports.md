## How Imports Work

Data imports are a four-step process: 

**1. Download the relevant import {{variable.templates}} from the {{variable.AdminPanel}}**.
**2. Fill out your {{variable.templates}} with your data.**
**3. Format the data in your {{variable.templates}}.**
**4. Upload the {{variable.template}} to the {{variable.AdminPanel}}**.
