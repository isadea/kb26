What is Passport by Nexudus?

Passport is a mobile app available on iOS and Android devices designed for your customers. 


The Passport that lets your customers:

  • Communicate with their community

Customers can message each other, add threads, post comments and search content.


  • Connect with other customers

The directory shows details from other customers of your space. They can see profiles and directly message them.


  • Book rooms

Customers can book any of the meeting rooms you've made available in your space via Passport. They just need to enter the date and time of the booking and the app shows them the resources available.


  • Purchase plans and products

Customers can sign up to new plans or purchase additional products. They also get an overview of the current plans and products they have on their account. 


  • Edit their profile details

Customers can access their profile at any time by going to My section on the app and edit the different fields as they need. 


  • Pay their invoices 
Customers can pay their invoices directly through the app from the Invoices section.
 

How Passport by Nexudus Works

Customers download the app on their phone and then log in using the same credentials they use to log in to your Members Portal. Once they're logged in, they can start using the app to make bookings, pay their invoices, communicate with other members or even edit their profile. 

Setting up Passport by Nexudus

All your customers need is access to the Members Portal and the app installed on their phone. Once they're log in using their Members Portal credentials, they can start using the app to book resources and edit their details.
