## What is Google Calendar (Customer)?
Google Calendar (Customer) is a way for your customers to link their Google Calendar with their Nexudus account. 

## How the Google Calendar (Customer) Integration Works
Your customers enable the integration from their account on the Members Portal and can then book resources in your space directly from their Google Calendar.
