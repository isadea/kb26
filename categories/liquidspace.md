## What is {{variable.LiquidSpace}}?
[LiquidSpace](https://liquidspace.com/){target="_blank"} is an online marketplace and workspace network for renting office space.

## How the {{variable.LiquidSpace}} integration works
You can avoid duplicate bookings between {{variable.LiquidSpace}} and {{variable.Nexudus}} by enabling the {{variable.GoogleCalendar}} integration from the Admin Panel and then connecting the corresponding calendars to {{variable.LiquidSpace}}. 
:::(Warning) (The integration between {{variable.LiquidSpace}} and {{variable.Nexudus}} is only possible via {{variable.GoogleCalendar}}.)
Bookings made in {{variable.LiquidSpace}} are synced in {{variable.GoogleCalendar}} that then syncs the booking in {{variable.Nexudus}}.
*LiquidSpace > {{variable.GoogleCalendar}} > {{variable.Nexudus}}*

Bookings made in {{variable.Nexudus}} are synced in {{variable.GoogleCalendar}} that then syncs the booking in {{variable.LiquidSpace}}.
*{{variable.Nexudus}} > {{variable.GoogleCalendar}} > {{variable.LiquidSpace}}*
:::


You need to enable the [{{variable.GoogleCalendar}}](https://help.nexudus.com/v3/docs/google-calendar-administrator){target="_blank"} integration before you can connect your calendars to {{variable.LiquidSpace}}.
