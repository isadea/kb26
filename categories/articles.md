## What are {{variable.ArticlesCAP}}? 

Your {{variable.MembersPortal}} includes an **{{variable.ArticlesCAP}}** section where you can post {{variable.articles}}. It can easily be switched on and off, based on your requirements. You can schedule {{variable.articles}} publication, add images, let {{variable.customers}} post comments, and even organize {{variable.articles}} by category. 

*For example, you can decide to share details about upcoming plans in your {{variable.space}} or simply post useful and entertaining content for your {{variable.customers}}.* 

![Articles_MP](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/Articles_MP.png){height="" width=""}

## How {{variable.ArticlesCAP}} Work
You can enable/disable the **{{variable.ArticlesCAP}}** section via **Settings > Modules > {{variable.ArticlesCAP}}** on the {{variable.AdminPanel}}. Once the section is enabled, you can create, organise and publish your {{variable.articles}}. 

## Articles and Wordpress

If you use a content management system like [WordPress](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/wordpress){target="_blank"} you can connect it to your {{variable.MembersPortal}} through methods such as:

* Embedding specific pages using iframes 
* Using a redirection plug-in
* Using the [{{variable.Nexudus}} APIs](/docs/api-getting-started){target=`_blank`}
