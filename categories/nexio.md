## What is {{variable.NexIO}}?
{{variable.NexIO}} is a Nexudus app available on [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/nexio-by-nexudus/id933010341){target="_blank"} and [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nexuduscheckinapp){target="_blank"} tablets that lets:

* Customers and visitors check in and out of your space
* Visitors register themsleves on the spot 
* Couriers deliver packages to customers and contact them over the phone 

## Tablet Requirements 
You need at least one tablet meeting the requirements detailed below to use {{variable.NexIO}}. You can choose to set up several tablets with {{variable.NexIO}} if you want to allow check-in/out in multiple areas of your space. 

{{snippet.TabletsRequirements}}

## Admin  for  {{variable.NexIO}}

You must use a full unrestricted admin to set up the app. If an admin ever needs to sign out of the app or change its location, they'll also need to: 

* Have access to the location associated with the device.
* Have access to the network location, if you manage more than one location with Nexudus.


## [Setting up NexIO](https://help.nexudus.com/v3/docs/setting-up-nexio){target="_blank"}
Before customers can start using NexIO to check in and out of your space, an admin needs to set up the app. 

## [Checking in/out with NexIO](https://nexudus.document360.io/v3/docs/checking-in-out-using-nexio){target="_blank"} 

{{variable.MembersCAP}} and {{variable.contacts}} can check in using the {{variable.Passport}}, a QR code, or their PIN code. 

Visitors can check in using the QR code or the PIN code they received in their registration email notification.

## [Registering Visitors with NexIO](https://nexudus.document360.io/v3/docs/visitor-self-registration-using-nexio){target="_blank"}
{{variable.VisitorsCAP}} who aren't registered by a {{variable.customer}} or an admin can register on the spot using NexIO. Visitors registering with NexIO are automatically checked-in as they register.
