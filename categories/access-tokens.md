## What are {{variable.Accesstokens_CAP}}?

{{variable.Accesstokens_CAP}} are codes that {{variable.customers}} and {{variable.visitors}} can use to connect to your Wi-Fi network for a fixed amount of time. 

:::(warning) (You must use **one** of our Wi-Fi integrations to use {{variable.accesstokens}}.)
{{variable.MoreInformation}} [MikroTik](/v3/docs/mikrotik){target="_blank"} or [RADIUS](/v3/docs/radius){target="_blank"}.
:::

## How {{variable.Accesstokens_CAP}} work 
Once you've enabled one of our Wi-Fi network integrations, you can start adding tokens to grant access to your network. 


The main way you can use {{variable.accesstoken}} is by creating them via on the {{variable.AdminPanel}}

## {{variable.Accesstokens_CAP}} for customers and booking guests 

Customers and booking guests get access tokens based on their purchases and the booked resource settings. 

{{variable.MoreInformation}} [Adding Access Tokens](/docs/adding-access-tokens){target=`_blank`}.

## {{variable.Accesstokens_CAP}} for visitors  
Visitors also get access tokens for the duration of their visit to your space. 
{{variable.MoreInformation}} [Adding Visitor Access Tokens](/docs/adding-visitor-access-tokens){target=`_blank`}.
