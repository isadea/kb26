## What is {{variable.Padword}}? 
{{variable.Padword}} is a door access integration developed that lets your customers unlock a number of doors based on their bookings, the passes they have, and their contracts for desks or offices. 

![Padword](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/Padword.png){height="" width=""}


:::(Warning) ({{variable.Padword}} adds a {{variable.Currencies}}50 charge to your monthly {{variable.Nexudus}} subscription.)
:::
## How {{variable.Padword}} works
All you need to integrate {{variable.Padword}} is a {{variable.Padword}} account and a {{variable.Nexudus}} account set up with the relevant [passes](/v3/docs/passes){target="_blank"}, [resources](/v3/docs/resources){target="_blank"}, and [offices & desks](/v3/docs/floor-plan-items){target="_blank"}. 

You can then connect your {{variable.Padword}} account to {{variable.Nexudus}} and match the relevant doors to each item.

## FAQ

### Does {{variable.Padword}} support check-ins/outs? 
No {{variable.Padword}} doesn't offer automated check-ins/outs in Nexudus as customers unlock doors. 

### Does {{variable.Padword}}  work with the {{variable.WLApp}}? 
It can work through automation tiles. There are some limitations to using tiles to unlock {{variable.Padword}} doors. For more information, check out [Automation Tiles](/v3/docs/automation-tiles){target="_blank"}.
