## What is {{variable.AzureADB2C}}?

{{variable.AzureADB2C}} is a single sign-on integration (SSO) that lets you offer SSO to customers who want their teams to log in using corporate credentials. Unlike the standard [{{variable.AzureAD}}](/v3/docs/azure-ad){target="_blank"} integration, {{variable.AzureADB2C}} lets you offer SSO to multiple teams using their respective active directories rather of relying on a single directory your space owns.
