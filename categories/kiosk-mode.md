[Kiosk mode](/docs/kiosk-mode){target=`_blank`} is one of two modes available on NexKiosk. 



![KioskMode.PNG](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/KioskMode.PNG){height="" width="500"}

Kiosk mode lets your customers make purchases via the app and pay either by card or adding by their purchase to their next invoice. 

Guests and visitors can also make purchases if you [connect a card reader](/docs/connecting-nexkiosk-card-readers){target=`_blank`}.
