## What is TopBrewer by Scanomat?
Scanomat is the world-leading manufacturer of premium, connected coffee machines. TopBrewer is their flagship coffee brewing solution that lets customers order, customise and pay for drinks using an integrated iPad. 

![ScanomatTopBrewer_Marketing.png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/ScanomatTopBrewer_Marketing.png){height="" width=""}


Visit the [TopBrewer website](https://www.scanomat.com/uk/products/topbrewer/){target="_blank"} to learn more about the different beverage machine options available. 

## How the TopBrewer by Scanomat Integration Works
Once the integration is enabled, customers order drinks using the iPad menu. As they scan the Nexudus QR code, their purchases are automatically added to their account in Nexudus and they are charged on their next invoice.
