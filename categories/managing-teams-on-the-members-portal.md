If you're a {{variable.teamadministrator}} you can add and remove people from your team in a few clicks on the {{variable.MembersPortal}}. 

Just click on **Dahshboard** and then **Team**. From there you can manage the access and [attendance](/docs/team-attendance){target=`_blank`} of your team in a few easy clicks. 

![image.png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/image%286%29.png){height="" width=""}

You can also click on the **Manage members** tab to [onboard](/docs/onboarding-team-members){target=`_blank`} and [offboard](/docs/offboarding-team-members){target=`_blank`} team members in a flash.
![image.png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/image%287%29.png){height="" width=""}

:::(Info) (You must be a [{{variable.teamadministrator}}](/docs/team-administrators){target=`_blank`} to onboard and offboard other members. )
:::
