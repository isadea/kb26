## What is {{variable.NexKIOSK}}?
NexKiosk is a simple and powerful app that turns your [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/NexKIOSK/id1517030719){target="_blank"} or [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nexpos){target="_blank"}  tablet into a fully functional point of sale. 



![NexKioskHomeScreen.png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/NexKioskHomeScreen.png){height="" width=""}

Whether you’re running a concierge desk, a café, or an honesty bar, {{variable.NexKIOSK}} helps you sell with ease and instantly syncs all transactions in {{variable.Nexudus}}. No complicated setup, no fuss!



## How {{variable.NexKIOSK}} works
{{variable.NexKIOSK}} can be used to sell any product in in your Nexudus inventory in a quick and easy way. 

Whether you decide to use it as a standard POS or have customers handle their own checkkout, and you can get it up and running in just a few minutes.
