## What is {{variable.GoogleBigQuery}}?
{{variable.GoogleBigQuery}} is a Google Cloud Platform service that lets you transfer data from your {{variable.Nexudus}} account to a data warehousing project in real-time. You can then build queries using SQL.

:::(Warning) (Using {{variable.GoogleBigQuery}} adds a **$/£/€100 monthly charge** to your Nexudus subscription per location enabled.)
:::

## How the {{variable.GoogleBigQuery}} Integration Works?

Once the integration is enabled in Nexudus, the data related to any record created, updated or deleted in Nexudus is automatically transferred to {{variable.GoogleBigQuery}}. You can then leverage this data in {{variable.GoogleBigQuery}}. 
:::(Info) (The integration must be enabled in each location that you want to connect to {{variable.GoogleBigQuery}} within your network.)
:::
