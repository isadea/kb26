The **Operations** section of the {{variable.AdminPanel}} is dedicated to managing day-to-day operations in your space. You can access the section by clicking **Operations** in the navigation menu of the {{variable.AdminPanel}}.

![Operations_GeneralPage.png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/Operations_GeneralPage.png){height="" width=""}

The section lets you manage the following sections: 

### [Customers](https://help.nexudus.com/v3/docs/customers)
### [Teams](https://help.nexudus.com/v3/docs/teams)
### [Visitors](/v3/docs/visitors){target="_blank"}
### [Bookings](https://help.nexudus.com/v3/docs/bookings)
### [Check-ins](https://help.nexudus.com/v3/docs/check-ins)
### [Help Desk Messages](https://help.nexudus.com/v3/docs/help-desk)
### [Deliveries](https://help.nexudus.com/v3/docs/deliveries)
