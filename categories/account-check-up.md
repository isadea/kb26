## What is the {{variable.AccountCheckup_CAP}} page? 

The **{{variable.AccountCheckup_CAP}}** page helps you stay on top of common operational issues that can impact your business. This includes issues such as: 

* Unpaid invoices
* Integration errors
* Low-stock alerts for products
* Key details missing details in a location 

Nexudus regularly runs a series of checks in each of your locations to detect issues. All issues Nexudus detects are added to your {{variable.AccountCheckup_CAP}}.

![AccountCheckupPath](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/AccountCheckupPath.gif){height="" width=""}

Account check-ups help you tackle issues before they actually impact your customers. Each notification includes the cause of the issue to help you fix it as soon as possible. 

##  How {{variable.AccountCheckup_CAP}}s work 


You can access this page via the **Dashboard** page of the {{variable.AdminPanel}}. Just click on the **Important items** tile and you'll be able to see all the current issues for the selected location. 


Once on the page, you can click on any of the notifications to get more information about the issues and which items need your attention. You can then easily fix the issue from the same page. 

*For example, if you get a low stock alert, we show you the products that are low in stock.*
