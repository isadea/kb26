## What is {{variable.DigitalSignature}}?
{{variable.DigitalSignature}} is an integration that lets you request legally binding digital signatures on any document you send to customers through {{variable.Nexudus}}. 

![DigitalSignaturePage.png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/DigitalSignaturePage.png){height="" width=""}


*For example, request a signature when customers sign up to plans, accept proposals, or receive any other document from your space.*

**Not sure if digital signatures are legally binding in your country?**
Check out {{variable.DigitalSignature}}'s guide on [international eSignature legality](https://www.hellosign.com/esignature-legality){target="_blank"}.


:::(Warning) ({{variable.DigitalSignature}} adds a **{{variable.Currencies}}15** charge to your monthly {{variable.Nexudus}} subscription per location.)
The first 30 signatures are included, and we charge **{{variable.Currencies}}0.50** per additional signature.
:::


## How the {{variable.DigitalSignature}} Integration Works
You can activate the integration {{variable.OnTheAdminPanel}}. As soon as the integration is active, you can start requesting digital signatures on documents you create in {{variable.Nexudus}}. You can also request signatures on external documents that you've uploaded to {{variable.Nexudus}}.


{{variable.MoreInformation}} [Integrating {{variable.DigitalSignature}}](https://nexudus.document360.io/v3/docs/integrating-digital-signature).

## FAQ
### Do I need to create a {{variable.DigitalSignature}} account to use the integration?
No, there's no need to open an account to use our integration.

### Can I connect my {{variable.DigitalSignature}} account to {{variable.Nexudus}}?
Unfortunately, you cannot use your own {{variable.DigitalSignature}} account with our integration.

### Can multiple customers sign the same document?
Yes, each document can be signed by several users through our integration.
