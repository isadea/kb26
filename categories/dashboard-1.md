## What is the Dashboard?

The **Dashboard** is the first page all admins see when they log in to the {{variable.AdminPanel}}. It's designed to give you an overview of key data point about your space to help you stay on top of things such as knowing who's currently in your space, important items that you should address, and more. 

![Dashboard_Page](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/Dashboard_Page.png){height="" width=""}


## Now Tab
This is the first tab you see when you log in to your {{variable.AdminPanel}} or when you click on the **Dashboard** section in the navigation menu. It gives you an overview of what's happening in your space that day. 

**Bookings Today**
Total number of bookings planned that day.

**Unpaid invoices**
Total balance of unpaid invoices for your location.

**Contracts Ending**
How many member contracts are about to end in the next 30 days.

**Contact messages**
How many contact messages are waiting to be read.

**Expected visitors today**
How many visitors are expected today.

**Checked in customers**
How many customers are currently checked in. 

**Open help desk messages**
Whether there are any open help desk messages.


## Membership Tab
This tab tracks your active memberships and their growth. 


## Finance Tab
This tab tracks your space's revenue and charts displaying your space's finances over the last 12 months.



## Occupancy Tab
This tab tracks floor plan occupancy in your space and lets you check your space's occupancy progression. 


## Bookings Tab

This tab tracks the total booking time in your space over the last month along with the total revenue generated by the bookings. 

## CRM Tab
This tab tracks how many opportunities you won and lost over the last month.
