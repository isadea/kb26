## What Is Included.co?
[Included.co](https://included.co/){target="_blank"} is an aggregator that helps your customers save money on products and services they need to grow by sharing discounts with 650 other coworking spaces, business centres, shared offices and accelerators.

## How the Included.co Integration Works
Once the integration is enabled, a new **Discounts** page is added to your Members Portal. You customers can then redeem the different discounts on products and services by visiting the page.
