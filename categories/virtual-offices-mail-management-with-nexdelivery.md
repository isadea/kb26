NexDelivery also includes additoonal mail management features that are only available to spaces subscribed to our [Virtual Offices](/docs/virtual-offices){target=`_blank`} add-on. 

## Mail management options available via Virtual Offices only
Virtual office customers can pick their mail management preferences. These preferences are automatically matched when you process deliveries for them using NexDelivery. 


### Document scanning 
Scan any document or mail sent to a virtual office customer. They automatically receive the scans via email and choose what to do with the physical documents and mail. 

[image]

{{variable.MoreInformation}} [Scanning documents with NexDelivery](/docs/scanning-documents-with-nexdelivery){target=`_blank`}. 

### Mail and parcel forwarding 
Forward mail and parcels to the virtual office customer's home address or a dedicated address. 

:::(Warning) (All forwarding labels and postal services are managed outside the app and Nexudus.)
NexDelivery isn't linked to any postal service and is solely designed to help you record that you forwarded mail or deliveries in Nexudus.
:::
