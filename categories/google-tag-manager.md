## What is Google Tag Manager? 
Google Tag Manager is a tag management system (TMS) that allows you to quickly and easily update measurement codes and related code fragments collectively known as tags on your website.

## How the Google Tag Manager Integration Works
Google Tag Manager helps you monitor the traffic on your Members Portal. Google Tag Manager is compatible with version 3 and 4 of the Members Portal.
