## What are {{variable.SaltoLockers_CAPS}}? 

{{variable.SaltoLockers_CAPS}} are lockers that your {{variable.customers}} can reserve on a rolling basis. They remain assigned to the {{variable.customer}} who reserved them until they cancel their reservation.

## FAQ

### Can I use {{variable.SaltoLocker}} without using {{variable.Salto}} as my main access control system? 
Yes, you can use any other access control system for your regular door access and only {{variable.SaltoLocker}} for your lockers. 

### Can {{variable.customers}} from the same {{variable.team}} share lockers? 
No, lockers are nominative and can only be used by the {{variable.customer}} who reserved them.
