## What Is NexDelivery?
NexDelivery is a Nexudus app that facilitates deliveries processing in your space. The app is available on [iOS](https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/nexdelivery/id1403149334){target="_blank"} and [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nexdelivery){target="_blank"}. 

![NexDelivery.jpg](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/NexDelivery.jpg){height="400" width=""}

## What You Need to Use NexDelivery 
- An iOS or Android device compatible with Bluetooth 4.0
- A steady WiFi connection 
- A full unrestricted admin account or an admin account with the [NexDelivery role](/docs/creating-nexdelivery-user-role){target=`_blank`}

## How NexDelivery Works
Once you've [set up the app](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/setting-up-nexdelivery){target="_blank"} on your phone or tablet, you're ready to [start processing deliveries](/docs/adding-deliveries-using-nexdelivery){target=`_blank`}. The app is designed to let you record multiple deliveries for the same customer in one go for speedy processing. 


You can also [process delivery collections](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/collecting-deliveries-using-nexdelivery){target="_blank"} through the app and automatically collect signatures. 

## NexDelivery and Virtual Offices 
 NexDelivery includes some additional features only available for Virtual Office customers. 
 
This includes document scanning, forwarding, and storage.
