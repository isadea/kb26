## FAQ

### Can {{variable.newsletters}} be customized to fit my branding? 

You can use the built-in editor to build {{variable.newsletters}} that meet your brand requirements. You can use the drag-and-drop elements or edit the HTML as needed.

### Can I cancel or call back a {{variable.newsletter}} I've sent?

You can't cancel or call back any {{variable.newsletters}} you've already sent. You can test your {{variable.newsletters}} before sending them to check for design or display issues.

### Can I send a {{variable.newsletter}} to several {{variable.subscriber}} groups at once?

You can send a {{variable.newsletter}} to 1000 subscribers at once. This can consist of several {{variable.subscriber}} groups or individuals. Your {{variable.newsletter}} may need to be sent several times to reach all your {{variable.subscribers}}. Contact support if you need to increase the limit.

### Can I schedule my {{variable.newsletters}} to send later?

No, you can't schedule your {{variable.newsletters}} to send at a future date. They are all sent manually from the {{variable.AdminPanel}}.
