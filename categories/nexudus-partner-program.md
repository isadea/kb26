## What Is the Nexudus Reseller Scheme?
The **Nexudus Reseller Scheme** is simple: you recommend Nexudus to people and you earn rewards once they sign up to Nexudus. 

Your reward level depends on how involved you want to be when onboarding new customers. We currently offer three reseller tiers. 

## What are the Different Reseller Tiers?
The Nexudus Reseller Scheme has three tiers that define how much you can earn and what's expected from you if you decide to become a Nexudus reseller. 

###  ![icon-affiliate-role-whistler.svg](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/icon-affiliate-role-whistler.svg)  The Whistler
Recommend Nexudus to someone you know and get 10% of their invoicing during the first year.

### ![icon-affiliate-role-advocate.svg](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/icon-affiliate-role-advocate.svg) The Advocate
Recommend Nexudus to someone you know and onboard them to get 20% of their invoicing during the first year.

### ![icon-affiliate-role-legend.svg](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/icon-affiliate-role-legend.svg)  The Legend
Recommend Nexudus, onboard and provide Nexudus support for someone to get 20% of their invoicing for the duration of their contract. 

## How You Can Become a Nexudus Reseller Partner

Becoming a Nexudus reseller is a simple three-step process: 

1. [Create your Nexudus Reseller Account](/v3/docs/creating-your-nexudus-reseller-account){target=`_blank`}
2. Sign the [Reseller Agreement](/v3/docs/nexudus-reseller-agreement){target=`_blank`} & Activate Your Nexudus Reseller Account
3. Start [Managing Your Customers and Payouts](/v3/docs/managing-reseller-customers){target=`_blank`}
