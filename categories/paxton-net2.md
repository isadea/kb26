## What is {{variable.PaxtonNET2}}?
{{variable.PaxtonNET2}} is an access control integration developed by Paxton. This integration lets your {{variable.customers}} access your space autonomously using access cards or fobs based on their {{variable.bookings}}, {{variable.passes}}, and {{variable.contracts}}. 
:::(Warning) (This integration adds a {{variable.Currencies}}50 charge to your {{variable.Nexudus}} subscription per location using {{variable.PaxtonNET2}}.)
:::

## FAQ 

### Can I use my {{variable.WLApp}} to unlock {{variable.PaxtonNET2}} doors? 
No, our {{variable.PaxtonNET2}} integration currently only supports **access cards** and **fobs**. 

### Can I use any kind of access card or fob? 
Yes, as long as the cards and fobs are compatible with {{variable.PaxtonNET2}}.

### Are cards and fobs automatically assigned to {{variable.customers}}?
Not quite. Linking your access cards to {{variable.customers}} is a manual process that you must complete for each {{variable.customer}}. {{variable.MoreInformation}} [Using {{variable.PaxtonNET2}}](/docs/using-paxton-net2){target=`_blank`}.
