The **Community** section of the {{variable.AdminPanel}} lets you build and foster community in your space. It also helps you collect customer feedback and share information with them.

![Community_Page(1).png](https://cdn.document360.io/4f9a66c7-3dbb-4052-97d8-5439302e1512/Images/Documentation/Community_Page%281%29.png){height="" width=""}

The Community section includes the following features: 

### [Articles ](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/articles)
Articles lets you publish content to a dedicated section on your {{variable.MembersPortal}}. 

### [FAQ Articles](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/faq-articles){target="_blank"} 
FAQ articles let you answer common questions through dedicated articles that are available to anyone with access to your {{variable.MembersPortal}}.

### [Perks ](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/perks)
Perks let you offer discounts and other types of perks in partnership with other businesses. 

### [Courses ](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/courses){target="_blank"}
Courses let you offer training on any topic of your choice to customers. 

### [Events](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/events-1){target="_blank"} 
Events let you create and sell tickets for events that your space organizes.

### [Discussion Boards ](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/discussion-boards){target="_blank"}
Discussion boards let your customers and admins have conversations on the {{variable.MembersPortal}}.

### [Surveys](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/surveys){target="_blank"}

Surveys help you collect and analyze customer feedback. 

### [Virtual Rooms ](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/virtual-rooms){target="_blank"}

Virtual rooms let your customers and/or their team use virtual meeting rooms to chat via videoconference.

### [Newsletters](https://help.nexudus.com//v3/docs/newsletters){target="_blank"}
Newsletters let you keep subscribers posted on what's happening in your space.
