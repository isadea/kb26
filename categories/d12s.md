## What are {{variable.D12S}} sensors?
{{variable.D12S}} are compact optic sensors developed by Disruptive Technologies. They are designed to collect data about your space's environment every 5 to 15 minutes.

*For example, track temperature in different areas of your space to manage air-con/heating or know if a locked door has been left open for more than 15 mins.*

{{variable.D12S}} sensors are a great way to optimize you day-to-day operations while making your space safer.
